Kauai, Hi
December 2015

Kauai Hi, where do we start? We visited the beautiful island of Kauai for our annivesary (3 years if you are wondering). There were places we knew we had to visit and others we stumbled upon. Here are a few of our favorite gems that are on this lush green garden isle. Mahalo!

  1. Travel the Waimea Canyon on Waimea Canyon Rd for a more scenic view
  2. Take a boat tour of the Na Pali Coast
  3. Lapperts Ice Cream is a must have we went multiple times!
  4. Bubba Burger (Double Teriyaki Burger is our recommendation)
  5. Huli Huli Chicken
  6. Puka Dog
  7. Wailua Shave Ice was another place we visited more than once
  8. The Shrimp Station (The Best Garlic and Coconut Shrimp on the island)
  9. Chicken in the Barrel
  10. Pono Market for a Hawaiian style plate lunch
  11. Must visit Hanalei Bay Valley and Beach Park
  12. Kauai Bike Trail 4.5 miles of scenic beaches
